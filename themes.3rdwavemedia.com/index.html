Why use our templates?

UX centred

We don't make "all purpose" templates. All the templates are designed with targeting users and business in mind. We research real-world examples before making the templates.

Mobile friendly

We believe that "mobile is the future" so all the templates are responsive and work across all mobile devices. Fully tested with iPhone, iPad and Android.

SEO friendly

We write semantic markup so search engines can "read" the pages with ease. All the code we release is valid W3C standard.

Highly customisable

We build all of our templates on the popular Bootstrap framework which means they are very easy to customise to meet your own needs.

Xiaoying is the UX designer behind all the templates. Every single template was carefully crafted by her from real-world example research to Balsamiq wireframes to element Photoshop design then to the final front-end code.
